EVER Working Paper Series

The EVER Working Paper Series contains basic research on economics of education, in particular on economics and management of vocational education and training (VET), including apprenticeship training.

The reported results are work in progress of the EVER network members. The Working Paper Series is listed in RePEc, and single articles are notified in SSRN Subject Matter Journals.
